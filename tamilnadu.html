History

The state was formed on 26 January 1950.
According to 2011 census Tamil Nadu has a total population of '72,147,030' people which got ranked 6th over all other states. With a density of 550/kmsq.
It has a total count of 38 districts with Chennai as its largest metropolitan Area.

Tamil Nadu is the most urbanised state in India, and one of the most industrialised states; the manufacturing sector accounts for more than one-third of the state's GDP.
