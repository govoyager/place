History

The state was formed on 24 January 1950.
According to 2011 census Uttar Pradesh has a total population of '199,812,341' people which got ranked 1st over all other states. With a density of 830 people/kmsq.
It has a total count of 75 districts with Kanpur as its largest city.

With over 200 million inhabitants, it is the most populous state in India as well as the most populous country subdivision in the world.
